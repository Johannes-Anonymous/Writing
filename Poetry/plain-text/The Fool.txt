# The Fool

The change was unveiled in winter,
under shrouds of snow;
From the heavens a pure gift,
a fresh start for our world below.

While darkness reached its peak
and outward silence was maintained,
the sword against all evil was unsheathed,
nine months after being made.

Unknown by crooked hearts,
unseen by the self-absorbed,
human weakness fused with strength Himself
so that death would be no more.

So began his final march,
the devil's exit unwittingly;
tricked he was, freed we were,
by Divine Charity.
