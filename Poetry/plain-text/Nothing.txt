# Nothing

I would give you my heart,
        but my blood has turned stale.

    I would share my dreams,
            but the rivers have gone dry.

        I would serenade the stars for us,
            but my lungs have cracked
                and collapsed.


    There is nothing
            I have to give,

        There is nothing
                I can share,

            There is nothing
                to be saved.


        I am dead,
            empty,
                hollow,

            surely forsaken
                and forgotten.


    Whatever glory was,
            or to whomever it was owed,

                remains lost,

                    unknown.
